ABOUT

SW3 Music are a UK based Artist Development & Songwriting Production Team who have been involved in the music industry for many years.

We have written songs for international artists and understand the importance of writing and producing commercial songs. SW3 Music has a team of talented songwriters and is always on the look out for new talent. We also have a music publishing division.
